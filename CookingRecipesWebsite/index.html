Steak

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened, divided
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 beef flat iron steak or boneless top sirloin steak (3/4 pound)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

HOW TO MAKE IT:

  • Rub the steak all over with a good lug of olive oil and a good pinch of sea salt and black pepper
  • Add the steak to a hot pan, then cook for 6 minutes for medium-rare, or to your liking, turning every minute
  • Halve a garlic clove and rub it over the steak every time you turn it
  • Rub the steak with a knob of butter â€“ the sweetness from the butter will make it taste divine!
  • Carve with a nice sharp carving knife, then serve with the resting juices drizzled on top

Popsicle

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1/3 cup peach schnapps
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 medium peach, sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • Fresh mint

HOW TO MAKE IT:

  • Mix the wine, peach schnapps and sugar in a pitcher. Stir until combined.
  • Place the fruit and mint in your popsicle mold.
  • Fill each mold with the white wine mixture.
  • Insert popsicle sticks, leaving room between the bottom of the mold and the bottom of the stick. Freeze overnight.
  • Remove popsicles from the mold and serve in a glass of prosecco!

Salmon

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 lemons, thinly sliced
  • 1 large salmon fillet
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 6 tbsp. butter, melted
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

HOW TO MAKE IT:

  • Line a large rimmed baking sheet with foil and grease with cooking spray.
  • Season both sides of the salmon with salt and pepper and place on top of lemon slices.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together butter, honey, garlic, thyme, and oregano. Pour over salmon then fold up foil around the salmon.
